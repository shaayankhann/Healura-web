FAQ

Common Questions

What do I need for an online consult?

A phone/laptop with camera, some floor space, and (optionally) a resistance band. We’ll guide you through everything.

How are plans delivered?

You’ll receive a PDF with simple daily/weekly tasks, plus quick video references. Follow‑ups are on WhatsApp/video.

Do you work with doctors’ prescriptions?

Yes. Share your MRI/X‑ray/notes during the first call. We coordinate the plan accordingly.

Refunds/rescheduling?

Reschedule up to 12 hours prior, refunds on case‑by‑case medical grounds.
